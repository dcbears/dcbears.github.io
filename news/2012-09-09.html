Chicago Bears Bar Expanding — Union Pub + Bar 20

In order to help accommodate the popularity of the Union Pub as home to DC's Chicago Bears fans, Union Pub is now including their sister establishment, Bar 201, located in the same block as Union Pub. (200 Massachusetts Ave, NE) as another recognized venue to watch the Monsters of the Midway.
