2020 DC Chicago Bears Fans Season at the Union Pub


Our friends at the Union Pub won't be hosting official game watch parties this season, but they do welcome everyone to come out and safely enjoy the NFL games on Sundays. Union Pub will do all that they can to accommodate requests, though due to limitations there are no guarantees to specific table or TV placement. While they are looking forward to hosting DC area Bears Fans for the 15th consecutive season, please know seating will be extremely limited due to the challenges and restrictions related to Covid. Reservations this season are strongly recommended. To book a reservation or learn more, please visit: https://unionpubdc.com/.
